So...Our Product?

Mindful Diagnosis will offer an implicit bias training program specifically for healthcare providers that uses a data-driven model to individually detect and correct personal biases.

What we will offer:

Simple quizzes based on real life cases allowing students to better mindfully diagnose patients.

Our course will be ACCME accredited, so physicians can earn 2.0 CME credits for their participation.

Helps reduce misdiagnosis rate as well leads to a life-long reductions in implicit bias.
